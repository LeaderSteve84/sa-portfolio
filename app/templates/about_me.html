About Me

As a self-taught enthusiast turned certified software engineer, i've always been driven by a passion for practical problem solving. My journey began with Python, and through dedicated and hard work, i earned a spot in the prestigious ALX Africa boot camp powered by Holberton Inc. This intensive training honed my skills as a full-stack software engineer, specializing in backend development.

With a strong passion foundation in programming languages like C, Python and Javascript/Node.js, i've also mastered various databases, frameworks and tools. My expertise includes:

  • Frameworks like Flask and Express.js
  • Relational databases (MySQL, PostgreSQL, etc), and non-relational databases (MongoDB, Redis, etc).
  • ORM and ODM using SQLAlchemy, Pymongo, etc.
  • Shell scripting, server configuration, deployment, and management.
  • Git and Github

Beyond my formal training, i've self-learned React.js, Bootstrap5.0 and PostgreSQL database to enhance my frontend skills, enabling me to operate as a versatile full-stack developer. Notable projects include an API recipe web application, real estate communication management system, my personal portfolio which is a web application, and lot more where i applied my knowledge and gained valuable experiences.

What drives me? The thrill of creating solutions to real-world problems and collaboration with like-minded individuals worldwide. In my free time, i enjoy motivational movies, hangouts, local news, and expanding my general knowledge.

My approach to work is guided by the philosophy that 'Challenges are around for one to succeed.' I break down complex problems into manageable pieces, ensuring excellent coding style and effective solutions. When faced with unfamiliar tasks, i embrance the mindset that 'i can learn to do it' which enables me to tackle new challenges with confidence. My unique strength lies in analyzing ideas and developing actionable plans to brings them to life.

As i continue on this exciting journey, my goal is to become a seasoned entrepreneur and developer of high-scale application solutions globally. I'm committed to excellence, collaboration, and continues learning.
